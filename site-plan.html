EDACHE'S EXCELLENCE PLUMBING

Site Name

EDACHE'S EXCELLENCE PLUMBING

This name represents a professional plumbing solutions company that provides industrial and commercial installations, maintenance, and emergency services. It was chosen because it reflects both the expertise of the owner (Edache Emmanuel Alexander) and the company’s commitment to excellence.

Optional domain availability: excellenceplumbing.com.ng

Site Purpose

The purpose of this site is to provide detailed information about plumbing services, showcase completed projects, and allow potential clients to easily contact the company. It will also serve as a hub for career opportunities, client testimonials, and references. The site builds credibility and makes it easy for clients to request quotes or emergency service.

Scenarios

  • How can I request a quote or schedule emergency plumbing repairs?
  • Where can I see examples of previous industrial and commercial projects completed by the company?
  • What career opportunities are available with EDACHE'S EXCELLENCE PLUMBING?

Color Schema

These colors will define the branding of the website:

  • Primary Blue (#004080): Used for headings, navigation bar, and buttons.
  • Accent Orange (#ff8000): Used for highlights, call-to-action buttons, and hover states.
  • Neutral Gray (#f4f4f4): Used as background color for sections.

Typography

Chosen fonts for the site:

  • Montserrat (Sans-serif): Used for headings and navigation links.
  • Roboto (Sans-serif): Used for body text and general content.
